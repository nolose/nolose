Theme Description

Next vCard is a Clean and premium online vCard ideal for businesses and freelancers. It's absolutely perfect for displaying your skills and experiences and to showcase your amazing portfolio. It is fully responsive, so you do not have to worry if your visitors are viewing your site on an iPhone, iPad or a computer. Next vCard comes with a home jQuery slider, a beautiful portfolio with filtering effect, resume and contact information with Google map.
